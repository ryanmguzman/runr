TRANSPARENCY

WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW

We're student-run. Here's an honest breakdown of risks and how we handle them.

LOW RISK
FOOD SAFETY
Pronto is a courier only. We transport sealed, restaurant-prepared food. We never prepare, store, or alter food at any point.
Legally exempts us from NC food service licensing. All food travels in insulated bags, sealed at the restaurant.
MEDIUM RISK
UNC CAMPUS POLICY
The university restricts commercial activity on campus grounds. Increased visibility could prompt attention from administration.
We deliver to building entrances only. We don't solicit on university property.
LOW RISK
WORKER CLASSIFICATION
Ambassadors are independent contractors who set their own hours, use their own equipment, and can decline any order.
Ambassadors earning over $600/year receive a 1099. We don't control schedules — a key legal distinction.
LOW RISK
PAYMENT DISPUTES
Venmo payments are typically final. Restaurant errors aren't our fault, but we'll always advocate on your behalf.
Every order confirmed via text before placing. Photo of receipt available on request. Our mistakes are always made right.
MEDIUM RISK
CAPACITY & WEATHER
We're a small team. High-demand nights may hit capacity before close. Severe weather may pause service.
Real-time updates on Instagram. No charge if we can't fulfill. Closures posted by 8:30PM on affected nights.
LOW RISK
BUSINESS & TAXES
Pronto operates as a legitimate NC courier business. All income reported. No food license required for couriers in NC.
NC LLC filing is $125 online. Business registration in progress. Ambassadors handle their own tax reporting.
