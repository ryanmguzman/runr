The Process

How it works

No app. No account. Fill out a form, get a text, food shows up. That's the whole thing.

Step 01
Fill the form
Tell us your restaurant, order, dorm, and Venmo. Takes under 60 seconds. No account or download required.
Step 02
Get confirmed
Text confirmation with your total and ETA within 5 minutes. We'll flag anything unavailable before placing your order.
Step 03
We pick it up
Order placed, food picked up fresh in an insulated bag. Second text when we're heading your way.
Step 04
Delivered to you
Final text when we're at your building. Meet us at the lobby. Pay via Venmo at drop-off — or send early if you prefer.
Group Orders
Ordering with 3 or more people? Submit one group order and split a flat $6.99 delivery fee — under $2.50 per person. The organizer gets 10% off their own item as a thank you. Just select Group Order in the form.

Transparency

What you should know

We're student-run. Here's an honest look at risks and how we handle them.

Low Risk
Food Safety
Pronto is a courier only. We transport sealed, restaurant-prepared food. We never prepare, store, or alter food.
Legally exempts us from NC food service licensing. All food travels in insulated bags, sealed at the restaurant.
Medium Risk
UNC Campus Policy
The university restricts commercial activity on campus grounds. Visibility could prompt attention from administration.
We deliver to building entrances only — not inside dorms. We don't solicit on university property.
Low Risk
Worker Classification
Ambassadors are independent contractors who set their own hours, use their own equipment, and can decline any order.
Ambassadors earning over $600/year receive a 1099. We don't control schedules — a key legal distinction.
Low Risk
Payment Disputes
Venmo payments are typically final. Restaurant errors aren't our fault, but we'll always advocate on your behalf.
Every order confirmed via text before placing. Photo of receipt available on request. Our mistakes are always made right.
Medium Risk
Capacity & Weather
We're a small team. High-demand nights may hit capacity before close. Severe weather may pause service.
Real-time updates on Instagram. No charge if we can't fulfill. Closures posted by 8:30PM on affected nights.
Low Risk
Business & Taxes
Pronto operates as a legitimate NC courier business. All income reported. No food license required for couriers in NC.
NC LLC filing is $125 online. Business registration in progress. Ambassadors handle their own tax reporting.
