Transparency

What you should know

We're student-run. Here's an honest breakdown of risks and how we handle them.

Low Risk
Food Safety
Rünr is a courier only. We transport sealed food from licensed restaurants — we never prepare, store, or alter food at any point.
All deliveries in insulated bags. This legally exempts us from NC food service licensing requirements.
Medium Risk
UNC Campus Policy
The university restricts commercial activity on campus grounds. Increased visibility could prompt pushback from administration.
We deliver to building entrances only — not inside dorms. We don't solicit on campus property.
Low Risk
Worker Classification
Ambassadors are independent contractors who set their own hours, use their own equipment, and can decline any order at any time.
Ambassadors earning over $600/year receive a 1099. We don't dictate schedules — both legally essential distinctions.
Low Risk
Payment Disputes
Venmo payments are typically final. Restaurant errors aren't our fault, but we'll advocate on your behalf every time.
We confirm every order via text before placing. Photo of receipt available on request. Our mistakes are always made right.
Medium Risk
Capacity & Weather
We're a small team. High-demand nights may hit capacity before close. Severe weather may suspend service for safety.
Real-time status on Instagram. No charge if we can't fulfill. Closures posted by 8:30PM on affected nights.
Low Risk
Business & Taxes
Rünr operates as a legitimate NC courier business. All income is reported. NC courier services don't require food licenses.
NC LLC filing is $125 online. Business registration in progress. Ambassadors handle their own tax reporting as independent contractors.
